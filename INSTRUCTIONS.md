# Instructions for Growth Journal

This guide provides instructions on how to use the Growth Journal application.

## What It Does

This application is your personal digital coach. It's a private and non-judgmental space to help you understand your habits, track your progress, and get insights into your own behavior.

Key features include:
*   **Journaling:** Write about your day, tracking your mood and energy levels.
*   **Goal Setting:** Define and track your personal and professional goals.
*   **Habit Tracking:** Build consistency with daily and weekly habits.
*   **AI-Powered Insights:** The app analyzes your entries to find patterns and offer personalized encouragement and suggestions.
*   **Git Integration:** You can connect local Git repositories to analyze your coding activity.

**Privacy First:** All your data is stored locally in your browser. It is not sent to any server, ensuring your journal remains completely private.

## How to Use It

The application is a web-based dashboard, accessible at **[https://behavioral-insight-journal-eilqdpttf-mtsalts-projects.vercel.app](https://behavioral-insight-journal-eilqdpttf-mtsalts-projects.vercel.app)**.

### 1. The Dashboard

The Dashboard is your home screen. It gives you a quick overview of your current stats, recent insights, and today's habits.

### 2. Writing in Your Journal

1.  Navigate to the **Journal** tab.
2.  Write freely about your day, thoughts, or feelings.
3.  Use the sliders to rate your **Mood** and **Energy Level** (from 1 to 5).
4.  Add tags to help you categorize and find entries later.
5.  Click **Save Entry**.

### 3. Setting Goals

1.  Navigate to the **Goals** tab.
2.  Click **"Create Goal"**.
3.  Give your goal a title, a category (e.g., "Health," "Career"), and an optional target date.
4.  As you make progress, you can update the status of your goal.

### 4. Tracking Habits

1.  Navigate to the **Habits** tab.
2.  Click **"Create Habit"** to add a new daily or weekly habit you want to build.
3.  Each day, you can check off the habits you've completed.
4.  The app will track your streak to help keep you motivated.

### 5. Viewing Insights and Analytics

*   **Insights Tab:** This is where the app's AI will post personalized observations about your patterns, celebrate your wins, and offer gentle encouragement.
*   **Analytics Tab:** Here you can see charts and graphs visualizing your mood trends, journaling frequency, and goal progress over time.

The more you use the journal, the more accurate and helpful the insights will become.
